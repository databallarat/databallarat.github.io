The Bulldogs in Ballarat

The Western Bulldogs have committed to play two to three AFL matches every year in Ballarat from 2017 on the delivery of an AFL standard Eureka Stadium.
The Victorian Government has announced $31.5 million to upgrade the Ballarat Major Events Precinct – including $15 million for Eureka Stadium to support the achievement of this aim.
Building on this commitment to upgrade Eureka Stadium – the State Government and the Club has entered into a five-year major partnership with the Bulldogs to support the Club in to bring football to the region and also suite of the Bulldog’s community programs.
This unique and exciting partnership will form the foundation for the Western Bulldogs to deliver on the commitment to build an enduring relationship with the Ballarat community and beyond.
Under the partnership, the Bulldogs will wear Ballarat-branded shorts in 2016 and will also roll out community initiatives such as the Club’s men’s health program, Sons of the West and youth leadership initiative, the Whitten Project.

Implementing our Digital Strategy

The City of Ballarat Digital Strategy was commissioned to help create a better future city. This hackathon will help the City of Ballarat meet the following key actions:

  • 1) Progressively make key data sets residing in Council open and available to the public
  • 2) We will host, encourage and facilitate events, competitions and processes that stimulate the development and release of new apps and digital services based on publically provided open data

Frequently Asked Questions
  • FAQ
What is a hackathon?

A hackathon brings people together that love technology and data. A hackathon opens up collaboration opportunities and allows you to extend your network in a fun and positive environment.

Who should register?

If you are a software developer, amatuer football analyst, software engineer, maker, story teller, someone who loves technology or loves to solve challenges then we would love to have you on board!

Teams

You are welcome to form teams before the event, join a team at the event, or compete as an individual. Experience has shown that teams are best limited to 4-6 people. We will endeavour to ensure that everyone who would like to be part of a team will be welcomed into a team at the start of the event. Remember this is just as much about fun as it is a competition!

What can I create?

You can create anything that has used our data in a clever and creative way! This could include websites, apps, sensors, 3D models, data visualisations, t-shirts, or anything that is only limited by your imagination!

Food, Drink & Accommodation

Unfortunately we are unable to provide accommodation for the event. Accommodation details for Ballarat can be found at Visit Ballarat.
Water, Coffee & Tea will be made availble during the event. Local cafes can be found within a 5 minute walk of the venue.
We will endeavour to provide as many meals as possible during the event. Meals provided during the event can be found on the schedule.
